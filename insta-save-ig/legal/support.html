Support — Insta Save IG

Effective date: 1 May 2026 Publisher: Zenith Marketing

This page explains how to get help with Insta Save IG, including subscriptions, refunds, technical issues, privacy requests, security reports, and abuse reports.

For legal terms, see the Terms of Service. For how we handle information, see the Privacy Policy.

1. Contact

Support email: aaron@zenithmarketing.com.au

When contacting support, include:

  • subject line starting with Insta Save IG;
  • your platform: iOS or Android;
  • the app version, if available;
  • a short description of the issue;
  • screenshots only if they do not expose unrelated personal information; and
  • for crashes or failed saves: approximate time, steps taken, and whether the issue involved quota, preview, purchase, restore, or save.

We aim to respond to routine support enquiries within several business days. Complex privacy, security, abuse, or purchase investigations may take longer.

We do not publish a personal residential address on this support page. Email is the primary support and legal contact channel.

2. What Insta Save IG does

Insta Save IG lets you paste supported links so the App can help preview and save media to your device, subject to:

  • device permissions;
  • platform restrictions;
  • link and media availability;
  • backend availability;
  • free-tier quota limits; and
  • paid subscription status.

The App is not designed to bypass Instagram security, private account restrictions, paid access controls, or content restrictions.

Private, deleted, restricted, protected, unsupported, or unavailable content may not work.

3. Instagram login details

Do not send us your Instagram password, login code, two-factor authentication code, or private credentials.

We do not need your Instagram login details for support.

4. Subscriptions, billing, and refunds

Purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. Subscription entitlement management may be supported by RevenueCat.

Manage or cancel a subscription

iOS:

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Insta Save IG

Android:

Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Insta Save IG

Wording may vary depending on your device, operating system version, country, and store account.

Deleting the App does not necessarily cancel a subscription. You must cancel through Apple App Store or Google Play.

Refunds

Refund eligibility is determined by Apple, Google, and applicable consumer law. Start with the refund flow in the store where you purchased the subscription.

If a successful purchase did not unlock Pro features, email aaron@zenithmarketing.com.au and include:

  • platform: iOS or Android;
  • app version;
  • approximate purchase time;
  • whether you tried restore purchase; and
  • a proof-of-purchase screenshot with sensitive details redacted.

5. Free quota and limit reached messages

The App may include a free usage limit, such as a limited number of saves in a rolling time window.

If you see a quota or limit message:

  • wait until the free quota resets;
  • check whether you are signed into the correct Apple or Google account for your subscription;
  • try restoring purchases if you are a paid subscriber; and
  • contact support if you believe the quota or entitlement state is wrong.

Reinstalling the App, clearing data, or switching devices may affect quota or entitlement behaviour, but these actions are not guaranteed to bypass limits.

6. Restore purchase troubleshooting

If Pro does not unlock after purchase:

  1. Confirm the purchase appears in Apple App Store or Google Play.
  2. Open Insta Save IG.
  3. Use the restore purchase option, if available.
  4. Restart the App.
  5. Contact support if it still does not unlock.

Include your platform, app version, approximate purchase time, and redacted proof of purchase if needed.

7. Permission troubleshooting

Photos, media, gallery, or storage

The App needs device permission to save media. If saving fails, check your device settings and allow the relevant permission for Insta Save IG.

Network errors

Check your internet connection. VPNs, ad blockers, private DNS, firewalls, or network filters may interfere with requests to the App backend or third-party platforms.

Link errors

Some links will not work because the content is private, deleted, restricted, unsupported, blocked, or temporarily unavailable.

8. Privacy requests

9. Security reports

Please include:

  • affected app version or endpoint, if known; and
  • any screenshots or logs that do not expose unrelated personal information.

Please avoid public disclosure until we have had a reasonable opportunity to review and respond.
